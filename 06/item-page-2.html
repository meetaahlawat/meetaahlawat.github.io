Camera Bag

Every photographer has their own individual needs and preferences, and mine has always been my ‘kata’ camera bags. I have carried these camera bags over my shoulder come rain, snow, sun or storm. They have roughly weighed about 6-8 kilos on an average day of shooting assignments. I absolutely cannot survive without them and carry them with me wherever I go. They keep my expensive photo gears safe and have little secret pockets where I stow away my memory cards, mobile phone, comb and a vaseline.
