Negatives

No I am not talking about negatives in a person or negativity as a feeling. I am talking about negatives of film. When I started learning photography, film based cameras were just about to go kaput. In fact, in April 2013, Fiji officially ended the production of motion picture film and in January 2012 Kodak filed for bankruptcy. I believe if ever my hard drive or computer crashes, I will still have some of my photography work because I have preserved my negatives.
