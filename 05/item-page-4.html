My Cameras

A recent addition to my camera collection is a Fuji instax polaroid camera, a gift from my husband. My collection includes two film cameras, a Nikon F2 and a Vivitar, two DSLR’s, Nikon D300 and D800s, two point and shoots and a polaroid camera. They are like my children, I love dusting and polishing them. They are my shining trophies of life. The sound of their shutter is music to my ears. IPhones are the snapshot cameras of today but the DSLR’s will be evergreen.
