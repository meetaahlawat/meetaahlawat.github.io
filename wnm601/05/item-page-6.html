Nikon 24-70mm f2.8 Lens

My most essential item in my life. My Nikon 24-70mm lens. I had bought it very lovingly from money I had been collecting since my student days. Its not that bulky yet does not have the feeling of a cheap plastic lens. It gives great depth of field and is very handy for portrait pictures. The 24-70 is an exquisite hunk of solid metal that just makes pictures with no fiddling. It feels as if it was hewn from a single solid ingot.
