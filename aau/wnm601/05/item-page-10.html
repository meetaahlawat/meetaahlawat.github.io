Tripod

I may not use my manfrotto tripod in most of my shoots but it is one item I must carry and have with me at all times. Its like an anchor to me, my sturdy built manfrotto tripod and monopod. My tripod only takes seconds to erect and adjust and can support my camera in the perfect position for however long I wish, helping me to take great images. And oh I may fall asleep during waiting around for hours for animals to make an appearance but my tripod will hold up and my camera will trigger the shot.
