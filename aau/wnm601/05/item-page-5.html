Flash

Recently my flash is something that I absolutely cannot be without. It is because I am shooting more and more wedding and indoor photos. This flash of mine literally saves my life. I detest it when lighting conditions are dismal but this flash does the trick. Actually if a photographer masters how to use a flash, he or she is the master of their trade.
