Odisha

One of the first things you notice about Odisha is the vast green landscape of the state. Farmers bent over in the fields tending to their crops. The niyamgiri lush green hills is home to ancient tribals called the dongria kondhs. They are amazing people and are adept land dwellers exhibiting greater adaptability to the forest environment. Hundreds of perennial streams flow from Niyamgiri hill, and there are hundreds of Dongria villages by the streams. The Dongria are considered the protectors of these streams, hills and jungles by the people of the nearby plains.

It is also known as 'the land of temples' as there are a number of temples that are beautifully carved and are a proud testimony to the empires that were established here. The land is also famous for its cultural heritage. The major attraction here is the classical Odissi dance form. Odisha has more than just culture and tradition.
