About

At 'experience incredible India' we like to tell about a place like it is, without fanfare or favor. All tourist websites usually show the most popular photos, but we like to share the experiences from off-beat culture and adventure travel. India is not all about the touristy spots, its about the farms, the common people, the streets, the streams, the villages, the occupations of villagers, the tribes and the very diverse culture spread out all across the nation.

India has 22 official languages. And according to Census of India, the total number of mother tongues spoken in India is 1652! India has 82237 newspapers, home to about nine recognized religions and about 122 major languages and 1599 other languages are spoken in the country! This makes India one of the most diverse lands found anywhere else in the world.

So we have tried our best to give you a glimpse into the wonderful world called India. Everybody can make their own travel and stay arrangements now a days, so we are not into bookings, but we are into information, so if you want to chat with us about any aspect of India get in touch with us and we promise to be available to you on the phone 24*7 and will get you as much information as it is possible for your adventurous stay in India.
